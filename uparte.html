[UP] Arte is the Faculty of Fine Arts Students’ Association official magazine.

[UP] ARTE is a periodical printed magazine that showcases works from the students and teachers of the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto. It was created in 1995, and in 2021 it was revived with the publishing of the fifth volume, in which I was a part of the editorial team.

The magazine aims to represent all types of projects related to artistic practices, no matter their theme or format. Thus, it’s through this diversity that the magazine finds and gains its own artistic identity.

Production

As a member of the editorial team of [UP] Arte, during the production of the fifth volume, I was responsible for the pagination of several articles submitted by students and teachers.

During the production process it was important to maintain a close contact with the authors of the different articles, since one of the main magazine’s goal was to make sure that the author’s vision for their work was respected and not distorted. At the same time [UP] Arte had to gain its own identity.

The editorial concept for the magazine was that the editorial rules set for it were meant to be broken in a different way in each pagination. Although grids and typography rules were established, they weren’t meant to be always followed. With this creation process, in the end the magazine had its own identity created through its own diversity and experimentalist touch.
